# Compliance Workflow

## Purpose

Evaluate OCI network posture against CIS baseline plus user-defined controls and produce prioritized remediation backlog.

## Steps

1. Confirm scope and control baseline.
2. Collect current state across network/security resources.
3. Evaluate controls and mark pass/fail/partial.
4. For each gap, assign severity and confidence.
5. Perform impact and rollback feasibility assessment.
6. Prioritize remediation backlog by risk reduction and execution safety.
7. Present findings and get approval before any remediation writes.

## Deliverables

- controls evaluated
- findings with severity/confidence
- risk summary
- prioritized remediation backlog
