# FastConnect and IPSec Workflow

## Purpose

Advise and validate hybrid connectivity design and troubleshooting for OCI FastConnect/IPSec.

## Checklist

1. Topology and redundancy model (single/dual circuits, tunnel diversity).
2. BGP policy, ASN correctness, route advertisements/filters.
3. MTU/MSS and fragmentation risk checks.
4. IPSec encryption/auth settings and tunnel liveness.
5. DRG and VCN route precedence and return-path symmetry.
6. Provider/partner handoff validation and operational runbooks.
7. Failover/failback test plan with measurable outcomes.

## Output

- validated hybrid design assumptions
- risk findings and remediations
- failover test outcomes and next actions
